He is an anthropologist of technology active in the field of contemporary cultures, interaction design and futures research. Nicolas is both Professor at the Geneva University of Arts and Design (HEAD – Genève) and co-founder of the Near Future Laboratory, a distributed network of design fiction pioneers.
